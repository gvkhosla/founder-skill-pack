# Homepage Positioning — Truth-First Founder Partner

## Category

Founder Skills is the brutally honest co-founder for people building with coding agents.

It helps founders go from idea to PMF with more clarity, pressure, and better next moves.

## Hero options

### Hero option A
**Turn your agent into a brutally honest co-founder.**

Founder Skills transforms your agent into a co-founder with product taste, validation rigor, and a growth mindset - to get you from idea to PMF and beyond.

### Hero option B
**Not a cheerleader. A founder partner.**

Founder Skills helps you find the hard truth, validate what matters, and decide what to do next — before you waste months building the wrong thing.

### Hero option C
**Tell the founder the truth before the market does.**

Founder Skills turns AI coding agents into clear-eyed collaborators that challenge weak assumptions, force evidence, and help startups build what actually matters.

## Supporting subhead

Use Founder Skills when the real problem is not code — it is clarity.
It reads company memory, tells the truth, writes the artifact, and names the next move.

## Core proof bullets

- reads company memory before speaking
- separates what you know, think, and hope
- asks the hard question you were avoiding
- gives a clear verdict: validate, narrow, build, or stop
- leaves behind artifacts your company can act on

## Key differentiators

### 1. Brutal honesty without bullshit
No generic praise. No fake confidence. No motivational slop.

### 2. Strategy and validation first
Founder Skills earns the right to build. It does not assume building is always the answer.

### 3. Company memory, not one-off chat
The system reads `.fs/` state, artifact freshness, truth memos, and founder context before it recommends anything.

### 4. One next move
Every session ends with one clear move, not a pile of suggestions.

## Partner section copy

### Section title
**Reads the state. Tells the truth. Calls the next move.**

### Section body
Founder Partner is the default starting point. It reads state and artifacts before it speaks. Then it names the hard truth and tells you what matters next.

### Proof bullets
- reads `.fs/company-state.json`, artifact graph, and recent truth memos first
- separates what is known from what is merely hoped
- asks one forcing question at a time when your story is fuzzy
- writes a `truth-memo.md` with a clear verdict
- routes into validation before build when the evidence is weak

## Tone guardrails

The homepage should feel:
- sharp
- calm
- uncompromising
- founder-native
- anti-hype

Avoid:
- generic "move faster" claims
- execution-first positioning
- agent swarm chest-beating
- "AI copilot" language
- too much emphasis on bundle generation before the core collaborator thesis is clear
